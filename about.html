The Company

Clothing by Orabelle is the finest edit in luxury fashion for women. We have over 200 of the best international designers, 600 new arrivals each week, fast and reliable delivery, excellent customer service, utmost security and data protection, and free returns and exchanges within 30 days.

We are an industry leader in the world of online luxury fashion and retail. For over 1 year, the highly successful Clothing by Orabelle store cemented its status in Munich as a pillar of style and taste. In 2022 it widened its customer base globally by launching a small e-commerce extension. Today, over 860 employees work together to create an unforgettable online shopping experience. What makes Clothing by Orabelle so unique? An exceptional curation of women's products from over 200 of the world's most covetable fashion brands; exclusive designer collaborations; an outstanding customer service team made up of native speakers in multiple languages; and fast and reliable delivery to more than 133 countries across the globe.
